This Privacy Policy explains how Dashly ("the App," "we," "us") handles information when you use our Android application. We built Dashly to work entirely on your device — please read below for exactly what that means in practice.

Summary: Dashly does not connect to the internet, does not send any data to us or to any third party, and does not use analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs. Everything you record or enter stays stored locally on your device, in your control.

1. Information We Collect

Dashly collects the following information, all of which is stored only on your device:

DataPurposeWhere it's stored
Name, email address, password To create and sign in to your local account Local device database only
Video and audio recordings The core dashcam function — recording your drives Local device storage only
Video metadata (file name, size, date, duration) To display your recordings in the app's gallery Local device database only

None of this information is transmitted off your device, to us, or to any third party. Dashly does not require or use an internet connection to function.

2. Permissions We Request

PermissionWhy we need it
CameraTo record video of your drives — the core function of the app
MicrophoneTo record audio alongside your video
Storage / Media (Video)To save recordings to your device and let you browse them in-app

These permissions are used only while you are actively using Dashly's recording and playback features. We do not access your camera, microphone, or storage in the background, and we do not use these permissions to collect data for any purpose other than the app's core dashcam functionality.

3. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transmit any of your data to third parties, advertisers, analytics providers, or any other party — because Dashly never sends data off your device in the first place. Dashly does not include any advertising SDK, analytics SDK, or crash-reporting SDK.

4. Data Storage & Security

All account information and recordings are stored locally in the app's private storage area on your device, using a local database and your device's file system. Because nothing leaves your device, your data is only as secure as your device itself — we recommend using your device's screen lock to protect access to the app.

5. Data Deletion

You are always in control of your data:

  • Delete individual recordings at any time from the Videos gallery inside the app.
  • Delete your account at any time from [Settings → Account → Delete Account], which permanently removes your account details and locally stored recordings from your device.
  • Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data, including your account and every recording, from your device.

6. Children's Privacy

Dashly is not directed at children under 13 (or the relevant age of digital consent in your region), and we do not knowingly collect information from children. Since the app requires manual account creation and is designed for drivers, it is not intended for use by children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, for example if we add new features (such as optional cloud backup) that change how data is handled. If we do, we will update the "Last updated" date above and, for material changes, notify users through the app or the Google Play Store listing.
